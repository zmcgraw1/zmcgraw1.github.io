About

I am an atmospheric scientist and global climate modeler dedicated to researching the impacts of aerosols and clouds on climate, currently a postdoc in Prof. Lorenzo Polvani's group at Columbia University as well as at the NASA Goddard Institute for Space Studies.

Download CV

Research

Current research interests include:

  • Impacts of aerosol-cloud interactions on global climate
  • Intensification of climate change by cloud feedbacks
  • Impacts of volcanic aerosols on temperature and precipitation
